Brown-Forman Silo Center in Louisville, Kentucky

Authored by: Sarah Roach

Introduction and Goals

The Brown-Forman Silo Center is located in Turkey Run Park in the Parklands in Louisville, Kentucky. This area of the Parklands was a former diary farm that has been transformed into a community space featuring the Pignic Barn, the Farmerâ€™s Table, Hockensmith Barn, and the 60-foot Silo Lookout. I wanted to highlight this unique area of Louisville that I visit all the time. I also wanted to learn more about this area's history and geography.

Map details

Created by Sarah Roach as a part of a final project in GEO 409 (Spring 2021) at the University of Kentucky Elevation data from the Kentucky's Elevation Data & Aerial Photography Program (KyFromAbove.ky.gov). Color aerial imagery from the National Agriculture Imagery Program (NAIP) Cesium Ion was used to create the slideshow with information and image sources from https://theparklands.org/ and https://www.josephandjoseph.net/ ArcGIS Pro was used to process LiDAR data and a Python script was developed to automate this process and is found in this Jupyter Notebook
